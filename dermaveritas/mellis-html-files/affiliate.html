Frequently Asked Questions

How much can I earn as an affiliate?
Your earnings depend on your marketing efforts and audience. With our 25-35% commission rates and an average order value of $150, top affiliates earn $5,000+ monthly.
When and how do I get paid?
We pay weekly via PayPal, direct bank transfer, or check. There's a minimum payout threshold of $100, and payments are processed every Friday for the previous week's confirmed sales.
Do I need a website to join?
While having a website helps, it's not required. You can promote our products through social media, email marketing, YouTube, or any other marketing channel that complies with our terms.
What marketing materials do you provide?
We provide high-quality banners, product images, email templates, social media content, and detailed product information. All materials are professionally designed to maximize your conversion rates.
Is there a fee to join the program?
No, joining our affiliate program is completely free. There are no setup fees, monthly fees, or any other charges. You only earn money when you generate sales.
